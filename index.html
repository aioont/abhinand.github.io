    About me

    Hello, I'm Abhinand I, an AI/ML Engineer from Kochi, Kerala, India, passionate about building production-grade intelligent systems that combine innovation with real-world impact. I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) from Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), and a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the College of Applied Science (IHRD).

    Currently, I work as an AI Engineer at Elint AI, designing and deploying Generative AI, NLP, and agentic AI systems across enterprise environments. My work spans LLM-powered RAG pipelines, prompt engineering, multi-agent orchestration, and cloud AI platforms including Azure AI Foundry. I also had the privilege of helping the cybersecurity startup Risknox.ai go from idea to MVP — a journey that culminated in them winning Best Innovative Solution at GISEC GLOBAL North Star 2025, Dubai.

    Earlier, I worked as a GenAI Developer Intern at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), where I built and deployed an LLM-powered voice companion for elderly care — optimising inference latency and integrating cloud AI APIs end-to-end. I bring a strong focus on Responsible AI: safety, explainability, and compliance are embedded in everything I build.

    Beyond engineering, I actively volunteer with Google Developer Groups (GDG) Kozhikode, mentor students at hackathons, and contribute to open-source projects. My project Kex (an open-source VNC server manager) has earned recognition from the developer community on GitHub. I also write about AI engineering on Medium and LinkedIn — sharing practical learnings on topics like LLM fine-tuning, agentic systems, and real-world AI deployment.

    I thrive on translating complex AI capabilities into scalable, compliant, and measurably impactful solutions — bringing engineering rigour, cross-functional collaboration, and a continuous-learning mindset to every team I join.
