    About me

    Hello, I'm Abhinand I, an AI Engineer and Web Developer from Kozhikode, Kerala, India, passionate about building intelligent systems that bridge innovation with impact. I’ve completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) from Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), following my B.Sc. in Computer Science from the College of Applied Science (IHRD).

    Currently, I work as an AI Engineer at Elint AI , where I contribute to developing advanced AI solutions that drive business intelligence and automation. I also had the privilege of helping the cybersecurity startup Risknox.ai bring their idea to life — from concept to MVP. Our team’s dedication paid off when Risknox.ai was crowned the Best Innovative Solution at the World’s Largest Cybersecurity Startup Showcase – GISEC GLOBAL North Star 2025, Dubai. This journey, from a whiteboard idea to a global stage, has been a defining milestone in my career.

    Earlier, I worked as a GenAI Developer Intern at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), where I built an AI-powered voice companion for elderly care. I’ve also led a team of developers to design and deploy AI-driven web applications, gaining hands-on experience in full-stack and LLM-based systems.

    I’m an active contributor to the tech community — volunteering with Google Developer Groups (GDG) Kozhikode, mentoring students, and participating in AI and innovation workshops. I also enjoy helping startups refine their ideas and build early-stage prototypes. My open-source project Kex has gained notable recognition with over 100 stars on GitHub, and I continue to explore creative intersections of AI and real-world problem-solving through projects like LegalGPT.

    My technical expertise includes Machine Learning, LLMs, NLP, Python, Django, and full-stack development. Beyond code, I value community, collaboration, and curiosity — believing that sharing knowledge and mentoring others is key to collective progress.

    Whether it's developing scalable AI systems, collaborating on research, or empowering the next generation of innovators, I’m driven by the vision of using AI to make technology more accessible, intelligent, and impactful.
